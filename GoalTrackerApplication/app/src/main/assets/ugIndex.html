Goal Tracker Application User Guide

Welcome to my Goals Tracker app by Joshua Watley! This app is made to help a uni student, or any adult for that matter with organising work or tasks that they have to do on a day to day basis.

The premise of this app is the idea of visually seeing what you have to do right there on your phone and being able to clearly see what is done, to be done and in progress.

*This app gets alot of its functionality from google sign in - without google sign in, you have to sign in every time you close the app! The test account is eee as username eee as password - in full release there will only be an option to sign in with google as the default entry is there for lecturer testing purposes*

Setting Goals

By tapping on a goal you will be loading up the goal viewer and you will be able to see details of the goals you have set including a description, dates due as well as time due. You can also see if you have a notifcation reminder on or off for the individual goal.

Here you can also change the goal status, either in progress or now complete.

Tapping this plus button will allow you to open the page for creating new goals. You will have to enter various fields in order to save the data about each goal you may have!

You will have to pull down to refresh the screen

Breaking Down Goals

By clicking the different sections of pie chart you can choose which category to sort your goals by.

When youve selected a category for sorting goals by clicking on a goal you can expand the goal to see more details about the goal and what it entails

If you expand a goal and want to change the progress of the goal you can do so and pull the screen down to refresh it and see how the breakdown updates!

Working with Motivations

By clicking this button you will be able to generate a new motivational quote to display on the screen from the on device database

By selecting on or off for this switch you are turning on the notification for your device, you will receive notifications every couple hours - motivations to help you get on with your day!
